Cybersecurity Labs (SQLi, SET, JTR)

Overview

A series of cybersecurity labs focusing on practical attacks and defenses, including SQL injection, password cracking, social engineering, DAC permissions, and system-level privilege modeling.

Labs Completed

  • Social Engineering Toolkit Credential harvesting using cloned login pages (Facebook, Instagram demos).
  • User/Group DAC Permissions umask analysis, ACLs, ownership transfers, and privilege restrictions.
  • Brute-force simulations 2FA security review & attack modeling.

Technologies Used

  • SQLMap
  • John the Ripper
